Getting Help
============


Bang
----
Search through the `mailing list archives
<http://sourceforge.net/mailarchive/forum.php?forum_name=bangproject-general>`_
or `subscribe
<mailto:bangproject-general@lists.sourceforge.net?subject=subscribe>`_ to
bangproject-general@lists.sourceforge.net and post a question/comment.


Ansible
-------
For questions related to ``ansible``, ``ansible-playbook``, playbooks, and
modules, see the `Ansible project <http://ansible.cc>`_ for `documentation
<http://ansible.cc/docs>`_ and several other support `resources
<http://ansible.cc/resources.html>`_.
